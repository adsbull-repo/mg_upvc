Types of uPVC Windows

uPVC windows engineered to provide maximum comfort and efficiency — reducing heat transfer and external noise while maintaining a stylish appearance.

01

Casement Windows

Side-hinged windows that open outward for maximum ventilation and clear outdoor views. Perfect for bedrooms and living rooms.

02

Sliding Windows

Space-saving horizontal sliding design, ideal for compact spaces. Smooth operation with no intrusion into room space.

03

Fixed Windows

Non-opening windows for maximum natural light and unobstructed views with excellent thermal performance.

04

Awning Windows

Top-hinged windows that open outward from the bottom, allowing ventilation even during light rain. Great for bathrooms and kitchens.

05

Louver Windows

Horizontal slats that tilt to control airflow and privacy. Ideal for tropical climates — excellent for South Indian homes.

06

Bay Windows

Projecting window sets that create extra space and panoramic views. Adds architectural elegance to any façade.

07

French Windows

Floor-to-ceiling double-panel windows that open like doors. Brings in abundant light and creates a seamless indoor-outdoor feel.

08

Arched Windows

Curved-top windows that add timeless elegance and architectural character to homes, villas, and religious spaces.

Why uPVC Windows?

Benefits of Choosing uPVC Windows

Discover why uPVC windows are the smart choice for modern Indian homes — combining energy savings, noise reduction, and lasting beauty.

Energy Efficiency

Multi-chamber uPVC profiles with double glazing reduce heat transfer by up to 70%, keeping interiors cooler and cutting AC electricity bills significantly.

Soundproofing

Reduce external noise by up to 45 dB with double or triple-glazed uPVC windows. Ideal for homes near highways, schools, or commercial areas.

Weather Resistant

uPVC profiles and sealing gaskets provide complete protection against rain, dust, and wind. Performs excellently in Tamil Nadu's monsoon season.

Low Maintenance

No painting, sanding or varnishing needed. Occasional wipe-down with a cloth keeps uPVC windows looking new for 25+ years.

Security

Multi-point locking mechanisms and toughened glass options make uPVC windows highly resistant to forced entry — safer than aluminium alternatives.

Aesthetic Options

Available in white, wood-grain, and custom RAL colours. Glass choices include clear, frosted, tinted, reflective and solar control variants.

Corrosion Free

Unlike steel or aluminium, uPVC never rusts or corrodes — even in coastal areas with high salt content in the air.

Eco-Friendly

100% recyclable at end of life. Energy-efficient performance reduces your carbon footprint every single day.
