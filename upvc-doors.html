Why uPVC Doors?

Benefits of Choosing uPVC Doors

uPVC outperforms wood, aluminium and steel in India's tropical climate. Here's why thousands of homeowners trust uPVC doors.

100% Waterproof

uPVC does not absorb water — no swelling, no warping, no rotting. Perfect for bathrooms, coastal areas and heavy monsoon regions like Tamil Nadu.

Termite & Pest Proof

Unlike wood, uPVC is completely immune to termites, fungi, and insects. No need for annual pest treatments or chemical sprays.

High Security

Multi-point locking systems, reinforced frames, and toughened glass options make uPVC doors significantly more secure than conventional doors.

Excellent Soundproofing

Double or triple-glazed glass panels with uPVC frames provide superior acoustic insulation — ideal for homes near busy roads or commercial areas.

Thermal Insulation

Multi-chamber uPVC profiles trap air to reduce heat transfer, helping maintain cooler interiors and reducing air conditioning costs by up to 30%.

Zero Maintenance

No painting, no polishing, no varnishing. uPVC doors only need occasional wiping with a damp cloth. The finish lasts 25+ years without fading.

Design Flexibility

Available in wood-grain textures, solid colours, and custom RAL colours. Glass options include frosted, tinted, textured, and toughened variants.

Eco-Friendly

100% recyclable material. uPVC manufacturing requires less energy than aluminium or steel, making it the environmentally responsible choice.
