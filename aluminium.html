Aluminium Windows

Types of Aluminium Windows

Aluminium windows known for sleek appearance and structural strength — allowing for larger openings and better natural light. Perfect for homes, offices, showrooms, and high-rise buildings.

01

Casement Windows

Side-hinged aluminium windows that offer maximum ventilation and clear outdoor views. Strong frames support large glass panels for modern interiors.

02

Sliding Windows

Space-saving horizontal sliding design with smooth movement. Ideal for compact areas, offices, and high-rise buildings.

03

Awning Windows

Top-hinged aluminium windows that allow ventilation even during light rain. Great for bathrooms, utility areas, and commercial buildings.
