Why Aluminium Windows?

Benefits of Aluminium Windows

Aluminium windows deliver superior strength, slim aesthetics, and long-term value — the architect's choice for modern commercial and premium residential projects.

Ultra-Slim Profiles

Aluminium's exceptional strength allows frame widths as narrow as 38mm, maximising the glass-to-frame ratio for maximum light, views, and a clean modern aesthetic.

High Structural Strength

Grade 6063-T5 aluminium alloy withstands high wind loads and spans large openings without deflection — essential for tall buildings and wide commercial glazing.

200+ Colour Options

Electrostatic powder coating in any RAL colour — from architectural silver to wood-grain — with guaranteed UV resistance for 20+ years without fading or peeling.

Corrosion Resistant

Aluminium naturally resists corrosion. Powder coating provides an additional protective barrier, making our windows suitable for coastal environments with high humidity and salt air.

Thermal Break Technology

Our thermal break aluminium profiles include a polyamide insulating strip that dramatically reduces heat conduction — improving energy efficiency without sacrificing slim aesthetics.

Large Span Capability

Aluminium windows support much larger panel sizes than uPVC, enabling floor-to-ceiling glazing and panoramic windows that would be structurally impossible with other materials.

Sustainable Material

Aluminium is the world's most recycled construction material. 75% of all aluminium ever produced is still in use today — making it an environmentally responsible long-term investment.

Low Maintenance

Powder-coated aluminium windows require nothing more than periodic cleaning. No rust treatment, painting or sanding — ever. A truly maintenance-free window solution.
