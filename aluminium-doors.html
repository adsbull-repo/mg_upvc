Why Aluminium Doors?

Benefits of Choosing Aluminium Doors

Aluminium doors offer unmatched strength-to-weight ratio, slim aesthetics and long-term performance — the preferred choice for modern commercial and luxury residential projects.

Slim, Modern Profiles

Aluminium's high strength allows very slim frame profiles — maximising glass area for unobstructed views and a sleek, contemporary look that uPVC cannot match.

Exceptional Strength

Grade 6063-T5 aluminium alloy provides outstanding rigidity. Ideal for large-span doors, high-rise buildings, and coastal applications exposed to strong winds.

Powder Coat Colours

Available in 200+ RAL powder coat colours with matte, gloss, metallic, and wood-grain textured finishes. Colours are UV-resistant and won't fade for 20+ years.

Corrosion Resistant

Aluminium naturally forms a protective oxide layer. When anodised or powder-coated, it withstands salt air and humidity — ideal for coastal areas like Chennai and Pondicherry.

Large Span Capability

Aluminium can support very wide and tall door openings — ideal for commercial and industrial applications where uPVC structural limits would be exceeded.

100% Recyclable

Aluminium is one of the most recycled materials globally. Using recycled aluminium saves 95% of the energy compared to primary production — a truly sustainable choice.

Low Maintenance

Powder-coated aluminium doors require minimal upkeep — just occasional cleaning. No risk of rust, rotting or peeling that affects steel and wooden alternatives.

Fire Resistant

Aluminium is non-combustible and does not contribute to fire spread — an important safety advantage for commercial buildings and multi-storey residences.
